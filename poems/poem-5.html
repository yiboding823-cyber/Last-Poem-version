独坐敬亭山

Sitting Alone on Mount Jingting

李白Li Bai · 唐代Tang Dynasty
众鸟高飞尽，
孤云独去闲。
相看两不厌，
只有敬亭山。
All the birds have flown up high;
One lone cloud drifts across the sky.
We gaze and never tire of each other's sight —
Only Mount Jingting, and I.
作者简介About the Poet

李白（701–762），号青莲居士，诗仙，一生纵横天下，晚年遭贬，孤独漂泊，将山视为知己。

Li Bai (701–762), the Immortal Poet, roamed the world freely all his life. In his final years, exiled and adrift, he found in the mountain a companion that truly understood him.

创作背景Historical Context

天宝十二年（753），李白政治失意，游历宣城，独坐敬亭山，万物皆散，唯山相伴，写尽孤独。

In 753 CE, politically disillusioned, Li Bai wandered to Xuancheng and sat alone on Mount Jingting. Birds gone, clouds drifted away — only the mountain remained. He wrote the mountain as his only true friend.

艺术特色Literary Style

全诗仅二十字，却写尽孤独与超然。以「尽」「去」写众物离去，以「两不厌」写人山相知，以山拟人，情感深沉而克制。

Only twenty characters, yet the poem contains entire worlds of solitude and transcendence. The departure of birds and clouds isolates the poet; 'never tire of each other' personifies the mountain as a soul mate — emotionally profound yet perfectly restrained.
