登鹳雀楼

Ascending the Stork Tower

王之涣Wang Zhihuan · 唐代Tang Dynasty
白日依山尽，
黄河入海流。
欲穷千里目，
更上一层楼。
The white sun sets behind the hills;
The Yellow River meets the sea and fills.
To see a thousand miles and more,
Climb yet one more floor.
作者简介About the Poet

王之涣（688–742），唐代著名边塞诗人，流传作品虽少，却首首精品。

Wang Zhihuan (688–742) was a celebrated frontier poet of the Tang dynasty. Few of his poems survive, but each is a gem.

创作背景Historical Context

诗人登临山西永济鹳雀楼，面对山河壮阔，写下此诗。鹳雀楼是唐代著名游览胜地，可望中条山与黄河。

Climbing the Stork Tower in Yongji, Shanxi, Wang Zhihuan faced the sweeping panorama of mountains and the great Yellow River. The tower was a famous Tang-dynasty viewing platform overlooking the Zhongtiao Mountains.

艺术特色Literary Style

前两句写景气势磅礴，夕阳与黄河构成壮阔图景；后两句由景及理，「更上一层楼」成为积极进取的千古箴言。

The first two lines paint a scene of breathtaking scale — the setting sun and the Yellow River. The final two lines distill this grandeur into a timeless maxim: to see further, one must always strive to climb higher.
