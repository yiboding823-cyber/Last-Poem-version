江雪

River Snow

柳宗元Liu Zongyuan · 唐代Tang Dynasty
千山鸟飞绝，
万径人踪灭。
孤舟蓑笠翁，
独钓寒江雪。
A thousand hills — no bird in flight;
A thousand paths — no footstep's sight.
One lone boat, a cloaked old man;
Fishing alone in river snow's span.
作者简介About the Poet

柳宗元（773–819），唐代文学家，唐宋八大家之一，因政治革新失败被贬永州十年。

Liu Zongyuan (773–819) was a Tang-dynasty writer and statesman, one of the Eight Great Prose Masters of the Tang and Song. After his political reform failed, he was exiled to Yongzhou for ten years.

创作背景Historical Context

永州贬谪期间，柳宗元以钓翁自喻，在极寒的雪天，以傲立寒江的意象，寄托政治失意后依然清高自守的心志。

In exile at Yongzhou, Liu Zongyuan identified with the solitary fisherman — standing firm in the freezing river, surrounded by absolute silence. The image embodies his refusal to compromise his integrity despite political defeat.

艺术特色Literary Style

二十字构筑一幅绝世水墨画。「绝」「灭」两字先清空天地，再以孤翁填入，以极度孤独衬托高洁，意境深远。

Twenty characters construct a perfect ink-wash painting. The world is first emptied, then a single figure is placed within the silence. Absolute loneliness becomes a declaration of moral purity — one of the most concentrated images of solitude in world literature.
