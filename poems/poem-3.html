山居秋暝

Autumn Evening in the Mountain Dwelling

王维Wang Wei · 唐代Tang Dynasty
空山新雨后，
天气晚来秋。
明月松间照，
清泉石上流。
竹喧归浣女，
莲动下渔舟。
随意春芳歇，
王孙自可留。
Fresh rain has washed the empty hills;
Autumn dusk fills the air it stills.
The bright moon gleams through pines above;
Clear springs flow over stones with love.
Bamboo rustles — washerwomen home;
Lotus stirs — fishermen's boats roam.
Let spring's flowers fade away;
A noble soul may choose to stay.
作者简介About the Poet

王维（701–761），字摩诘，世称诗佛，苏轼评其「诗中有画，画中有诗」。

Wang Wei (701–761), styled Mojie, was called the Buddha of Poetry. Su Shi praised him: 'In his poems there is painting; in his paintings there is poetry.'

创作背景Historical Context

王维晚年隐居蓝田辋川，此诗描绘山中秋暮雨后景象，借景抒怀，表达对田园隐逸生活的热爱与向往。

In his later years, Wang Wei retired to his Wangchuan estate in Lantian. This poem captures the mountain after an autumn evening rain, expressing his deep love for the reclusive life amid nature.

艺术特色Literary Style

颔联「明月松间照，清泉石上流」是中国山水诗中最完美的意象对仗之一，声色俱全，动静相宜，被誉为诗中画卷。

The couplet 'bright moon through pines / clear springs over stones' is among the most celebrated paired images in Chinese landscape poetry — balancing light and sound, stillness and motion in perfect harmony.
