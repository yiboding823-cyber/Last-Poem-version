早发白帝城

Departing White Emperor City at Dawn

李白Li Bai · 唐代Tang Dynasty
朝辞白帝彩云间，
千里江陵一日还。
两岸猿声啼不住，
轻舟已过万重山。
At dawn I leave White Emperor amid rosy cloud;
A thousand miles to Jiangling — one day's proud.
Apes on both banks cry without cease;
My light boat has passed ten thousand peaks.
作者简介About the Poet

李白因参与永王事件被流放夜郎，行至白帝城时突获赦免，喜出望外，顺江东下，写下此诗。

Li Bai had been exiled for his involvement in the Prince Yong affair. Upon receiving a sudden pardon at White Emperor City, he turned his boat eastward down the Yangtze, overwhelmed with joy, and wrote this poem.

创作背景Historical Context

唐肃宗乾元二年（759），李白获赦，自白帝城乘舟顺流而下。峡江两岸猿声回响，三峡风光衬托诗人的喜悦之情。

In 759 CE, newly pardoned, Li Bai sailed from White Emperor City downstream through the Three Gorges. The apes' echoing cries along the steep cliffs frame the poet's overwhelming sense of liberation and joy.

艺术特色Literary Style

全诗以速度写喜悦，「一日还」写船行之快，「轻舟已过万重山」将空间感与情感融为一体，是李白最传神的绝句之一。

The poem uses speed as an expression of joy. 'One day' conveys the boat's swiftness; 'ten thousand peaks already passed' fuses spatial sensation with emotional exhilaration. It is one of Li Bai's most kinetically alive quatrains.
