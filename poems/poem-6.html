终南山

The Zhongnan Mountains

王维Wang Wei · 唐代Tang Dynasty
太乙近天都，
连山接海隅。
白云回望合，
青霭入看无。
分野中峰变，
阴晴众壑殊。
欲投人处宿，
隔水问樵夫。
Taiyi peak nears heaven's capital town;
Its ridges stretch to where the seas go down.
White clouds close behind when I turn back;
Blue mist dissolves when entered — leaves no track.
The middle peak divides star territories;
Each valley holds its own sun and shadow's glories.
To find a place to rest as evening falls,
Across the stream I ask the woodsman's calls.
作者简介About the Poet

王维长期隐居终南山辋川，此诗以宏大视角描绘终南山的磅礴气势与神秘变幻。

Wang Wei lived for many years in retreat in the Zhongnan Mountains. This poem surveys the range with sweeping grandeur and captures its mysterious, ever-changing atmosphere.

创作背景Historical Context

此诗创作于王维隐居终南山时期，诗人以俯瞰之势描绘山脉全貌，由宏观到微观，由远及近，层次分明。

Written during Wang Wei's reclusive years in the Zhongnan range, the poem surveys the mountains from a panoramic height and gradually descends to the intimate human detail of asking a woodcutter for shelter at dusk.

艺术特色Literary Style

颔联「白云回望合，青霭入看无」以云雾的聚散描绘山中变幻，虚实相生，是山水诗中描写云雾的绝妙名句。

The couplet about white clouds closing behind and blue mist disappearing within is a masterstroke of depicting mountain atmosphere — clouds both real and illusory, the landscape itself breathing and shifting around the traveler.
